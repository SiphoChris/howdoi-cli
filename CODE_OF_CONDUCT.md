# Code of Conduct

## Our commitment

howdoi is a community project. We are committed to making participation a welcoming experience for everyone, regardless of experience level, background, or identity.

## Expected behaviour

- Be kind and respectful in all interactions
- Give and receive constructive feedback gracefully
- Focus on what is best for the community and the project
- Assume good intent — most people are here to help

## Unacceptable behaviour

- Harassment, insults, or personal attacks
- Dismissing contributions because of who made them
- Any form of discrimination

## Reporting

If you experience or witness unacceptable behaviour, open a private issue or contact the maintainer directly via GitHub.

## Enforcement

Violations may result in removal from the project at the maintainer's discretion.
